# Test Cases – Product Listing

---

### TC_PRODUCT_01

**Title:** Display product list after login
**Priority:** High

**Steps:**

1. Login with valid credentials

**Expected Result:**

- Product list is displayed
- Each product has name, price, and image

---

### TC_PRODUCT_02

**Title:** View product details
**Priority:** Medium

**Steps:**

1. Click on a product

**Expected Result:**

- User is navigated to product detail page
- Product information is displayed correctly

---

### TC_PRODUCT_03

**Title:** Product list consistency after refresh
**Priority:** Medium

**Steps:**

1. Login
2. Refresh page

**Expected Result:**

- Product list remains unchanged

---

### TC_PRODUCT_04

**Title:** Sort products by different options
**Priority:** Medium

**Steps:**

1. Select sort option (e.g., Price Low to High)

**Expected Result:**

- Products are sorted correctly based on selected option

---

### TC_PRODUCT_05

**Title:** Navigate back from product detail
**Priority:** Medium

**Steps:**

1. Open product detail
2. Click back button

**Expected Result:**

- User returns to product list page
- Product list is displayed correctly
